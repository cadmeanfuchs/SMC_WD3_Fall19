UX Highlights

Curiator

The geographically remote campuses of SMC needed a solution: how to make a far-flung student body one cohesive community. Curiator stepped into that ring, offering a collaborative platform that prioritized projects over profiles

Amazon Shelf

Despite corning the market, Amazon's reading experience lacks magic. Shelf integrates the various Amazon platforms into one vivacious experience, aiding user's hunt for new material, while giving their collections some pinache.

Agora

Agora is a study-location app built on myriad research and interviews. Leveraging our users' knowledge, Agora offers a tailored approach to finding the perfect place to take notes, hold a group meeting, or cozy up with a book.

Typography Highlights

Tinker is an Adler-based monospace that seeks to bring play to a style that's often and needlessly eschewed joy for order.

Parlance is a monocase that blends elegant modulation and character-rich swishes to convey refinement without pompacity.

Assorted Design

Meringue Puff

Made using Kris Choi's marvelous typeface, Meringue, these posters were a collaboration between Timothy Dutch and I. There are few better uses of design than helping promote wonderful things for people to enjoy.

UF Branding

Hiho! It's time for a creative to whinge about identity. This is a sketch to print overview of the long road to bringing my brand design to being. As a bonus, you'll see the early development of this site's typeface, Parlance.

Clic Branding

Alongside a small team, I was tasked with designing a logo and brand identity for a new organization service. After completing the core identity, I assisted my design team in deploying it across a wide array of products and mediums.
