Clic Organization

Previous Next

My team did the initial branding and design for a local organization startup. We sat down to interview the owner and found out a little about her, before we began.

She was earthy, grounded, and touched the world lightly. With subtle pops of color, you could see the airy atmosphere of her brand in the very way she dressed. As such, Clic had to feel open and appealing.

With a logo and color system designed to work on numerous pallets and textures, we wanted to think beyond the screen and page. We felt it was important that Clic could stay with her customers after she left, so we ensured we could leave them with an assortment of organization-building branded tools.

This is documentation of our first pass at the project, with later revisions being done privately.
