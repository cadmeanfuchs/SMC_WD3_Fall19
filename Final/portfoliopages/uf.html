Designing Urban Fuchs

Previous Next

Summarizing yourself can be the hardest challenge put before any creative. I am a set of intuitive pumps, chemical sluries, and perfectly odd stimuli. Welcome back out, stuff I create. We'd lose a year diagnosing how any one of you got there.

So, there's got to be a certain mystique in the brand, and a certain etherealness. Some sort of otherworld bookishness, with crisp modernity tossed in. Updated Dada?

Well, I tend to like what I worked out. There's easter eggs all over, and a dual-logo system that works quite nicely in a lot of applications. But my favorite aspect was being able to settle on a thing, which I could put from my hands to yours, and say, "Look, this is me."
