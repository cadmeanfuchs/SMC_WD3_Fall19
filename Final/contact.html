FAQ's

Are you able to do short turn arounds?

Woah, boy! That's a vague question. Generally, I'm pretty speedy - but, the more specific you can be about time expectations, the clearer we can talk.

Do you work with people outside LA?

Absolutely. It's 2019, and I've finally figured out FaceTime. Whether you're a phone, email, or video person, I'll work with you to make sure we can communicate well.

What rates could I expect for. . .

When you ask this, keep in mind that I will use industry-standard rules around revisions and rush times. These circumstances can preclude flat-rate quotes. I will always quote you a projection alongside possible additional rates, even if you never expect to use them - just in case you do!

Your work has a style. Are you comfortable with a different aesthetic?

You've got me pegged, human! I do have a style, but don't think I'm nervous around your chosen aesthetic. This work represents a cohesive glimpse, not a full breadth of possibilities. Check out my Instagram and Behance for those.

How do you pronounce your name?

Soph like 'sofa' with no a. Fuchs like fox. Or, you can try to really nail 'between-an-aw-and-a-uh-sound,' but you'll just say a naughty word. So, like fox works.
